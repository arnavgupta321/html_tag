Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madurai, India, and studied engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology in Kharagpur. He earned a master's degree from Stanford University and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. Pichai's early includes positions at Allied Materials and McKinsey & Co. Gaining Experience In 1997, Sundar Pichai abandoned the Ph.D. program at Stanford University, accepting his master's degree in material sciences and engineering. He joined Allied Materials, a semiconductor manufacturer in Silicon Valley, as a product manager before leaving to pursue an MBA at the University of Pennsylvania. After graduation in 2002, Pichai accepted a position as a management consultant at McKinsey & Co.
